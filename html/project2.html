The Pilgrims

The story is told through seven different pilgrims.

Each character shares their own story during the journey.

List of Pilgrims

  1. The Priest
  2. The Soldier
  3. The Poet
  4. The Scholar
  5. The Detective
  6. The Consul
  7. The Templar

Why This Book Is Unique

The book is written in a style similar to The Canterbury Tales.

Each character tells their own story, which builds the overall plot, and boy what a PLOT!
